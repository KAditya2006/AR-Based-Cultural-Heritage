Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AR experience work?

+

Our AR experience uses your device's camera and advanced web technologies to overlay 3D models of heritage sites onto your real environment. Simply visit our Explore page, select a monument, and click "Enter AR Mode" to start exploring!

Is the platform free to use?

+

Yes! Our AR Cultural Heritage Platform is completely free to use. We believe in making India's cultural heritage accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Which devices are supported?

+

Our platform works on modern smartphones, tablets, and computers with updated browsers. For the best AR experience, we recommend using a mobile device with AR capabilities.

Can I contribute content or suggest new heritage sites?

+

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from heritage enthusiasts, historians, and cultural experts. Please contact us with your suggestions or content contributions.

How accurate is the historical information?

+

All our content is researched and verified by heritage experts and historians. We strive for accuracy and regularly update our information based on the latest archaeological and historical findings.

Do you offer educational partnerships?

+

Yes! We partner with schools, universities, and educational institutions to provide immersive heritage learning experiences. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.
